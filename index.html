FAQ

How do I get started as a designer?

Start by learning design basics, exploring tools like Photoshop or Figma, and building a portfolio with small projects or practice designs.

Can I work remotely as a designer?

Absolutely! The world is your oyster! Many companies are looking for talented designers to work from anywhere, even your couch!

What’s the salary for a beginner?

Beginner salaries vary by location, but they typically range from $10,000 to $30,000 annually, depending on skills and industry.

What is UI/UX design?

UI (User Interface) design focuses on how a product looks, while UX (User Experience) design ensures it's easy and enjoyable to use. Both aim to improve user satisfaction. UI/UX design is the art of making digital experiences delightful and user-friendly. Think of it as the magic wand that transforms boring interfaces into engaging adventures!
